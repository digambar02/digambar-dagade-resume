Linear Regression as Geometry

Published: Jan 2025

Image source: Pixabay

Linear regression is often taught as a formula, but it is fundamentally a geometric projection problem.

The Core Equation

$$ \hat{\beta} = (X^TX)^{-1}X^Ty $$

This equation finds the point in column space of \(X\) closest to \(y\).

Key Insight

  • Response vector lives in \( \mathbb{R}^n \)
  • Predictors span a subspace
  • OLS = orthogonal projection
